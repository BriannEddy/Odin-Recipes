Arroz Con Leche

Description

Learn how to make arroz con leche right now!

This recipe will teach you how to make rice pudding or arroz con leche with ease. Thanks to the recipe of Lorena Guzman, we were able to share this with you! Eat is as a snack, late night meal, or breakfast, this multipurpose recipe will satisft you and your stomach!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of rice
  • 6 Cups of water
  • 3/4 gallon of milk
  • 2 12oz cans of Condensed milk
  • Cinamon Sticks
  • Optional: Cinamon powder

Steps

  1. Cook 2 cups of rice in 6 cups of water on medium until rice is soft
  2. Add 3/4 gallon of milk into rice once water is gone
  3. Add condensed milk to add sweetness
  4. Add 2 sitkcs of cinamon to rice
  5. Add 3-4 cups of granualted sugar
  6. Once served, add cinamon powder is wanted to extra flavor!
