Tropical Burner Smoothie

Description

This liquado (smoothie) will give you great health benefits including:
  • Immune System Support
  • Digestion improvements
  • Skin clearing
  • anti-inflamatory benefits
Let this drink be a great start to your day as it is easy to make and delicious to drink. You will feel great and ready for whatever the day throws at you.

Ingredients

  • 3 cupf of water
  • 3 handfulls baby spinach
  • 2 tbsp ground flaxseeds
  • 2 cups pinneapple chunks
  • 2 cups mango chunks
  • 1 large orange

Steps

  1. Add the water, baby spinach, and ground flaxseeds into blender on hight for 1 minute
  2. Add pinneapple chunks, mango chunks, and large orange into the blender on high for 1 minute
  3. Test and add stevia to sweeten if required
